9.2: Describe accessibility compliance (including ADA and section 508) responsibilities in Website development

  • ADA
    • Americans with Disabilities Act
    • states that all "electronic and information technology" must be accessible to people with disabilities.
    • People who need to follow:
      • State and local government agencies
      • private employers with 15 or more employees
      • businesses that operate for the benefit of the public
  • Section 508
    • part of the rehabilitation act of 1973.
    • affect federal agencies and contractors/vendors associated with such agencies
    • Most important parts:
      • Section 255 of the Communications Act:
        • telecommunications products/services should be accessible
      • 21st Century Communications and Video Accessibility Act:
        • Advanced communications products/services should be accessible
      • Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)
        • States that there should be no discrimination against disabled individuals
